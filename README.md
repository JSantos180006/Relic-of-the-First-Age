# Relic-of-the-First-Age
Text-Based Dungeon Crawler 


A text-based adventure where our hero or the player discovers an odd and metal looking dungeon. The story takes place in a fantasy world where people use candles to light the room and fight using magic and swords. This is a ATB combat adventure.


## Features
 - ATB-Style Combat System
 - Endless Monster Attacks
 - Auto-Attack Toggle
 - Class System
 - EXP & Leveling System
 - Stat Point Allocation
 - Loot Drop System & Equipment
 - Enemy Rarities & Scaling
 - Inventory & Equipment UI
 - Generated Encounters

## Playable Classes

## Warrior
- High Attack & Defense
- Berserker Passive
- Chance to deal high damage at the cost of defense

## Mage
- High Intelligence = High EXP Gain
- Chance to gain Luck Stat on level up

## Priest
- High Faith and Defense
- Passive HP Regeneration during combat


## Combat System
- Active Time Battle meter-based combat
- Every Turn is based on Attack Speed
- Player and Enemy act independently 
- Player can Pause or Resume Runs
- Combat flows continuously 


## Leveling & Stat System
- Players earn EXP for defeating Monsters
- Every Monster drops different amounts of EXP depending on rarity
- Every Level Up gives the player stats to allocate


## Equipment & Loot
- All monsters have a chance to drop loot
- Equipment automatically applies bonuses to stats when equipped
- Equipment includes Helmets, Chestplates, Gauntlets, Greaves, Primary Weapons, Offhand Weapons, and 2 Accessory Slots
- Equipment can apply bonuses to ATK, DEF, INT, FTH, LCK, ASPD, and HP
- Equipment is seperated into multiple rarities common, uncommon, rare, legendary, mythical, and mastercraft


## Monsters
- There are multiple rarities of monsters from common, uncommon, rare, legendary, and mythical
- How rare the monster scales the difficulty of the monster 


## Controls
Up Arrow & Down Arrow = Navigate Menus

ENTER = Select / Equip

ESC = Exit Inventory

Start / Pause Run = Begins / Stops Combat Cycle

Attack = Toggle Auto-Attack

Run = Attempt Escape

Quit = Exit Dungeon / Quit Game
